My Learning Style

  • After taking the quiz, I have learned that I am a predominantly Tactile, with some points in Auditory. This tells me that I prefer to be hands-on but I can also use listening tools to learn and study as well.

  • From EducationPlanner.org for tactile, "Tactile learners prefer hands-on activities, physical movement, and learning through touch." Regarding auditory, "As an auditory learner, you grasp information best through hearing... Reading aloud helps you learn, and you might hum or talk when bored."

  • Based on what I read, it helped me realize that some of the little quirks, such as making noise while bored and fidgeting when I sit too long are traits of my learning style. Using what I learned, I will be utilizing the Pomodoro timer to maintain focus while also allowing me the time to get up and stretch when needed. As this class is quite hands on (as seen per this homework), I am already doing some of the things a tactile learner would do to help them learn more effectively.
